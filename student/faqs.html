  • How should I save money?

    1. The first step to start saving money is to figure out how much you spend. Keep track of all your expenses—that means every coffee, household item and cash tip.

    2. Once you have an idea of what you spend in a month, you can begin to organize your recorded expenses into a workable budget.

    3. If your expenses are so high that you can’t save as much as you’d like, it might be time to cut back.

    4. One of the best ways to save money is to set a goal.

    5. After your expenses and income, your goals are likely to have the biggest impact on how you allocate your savings.

    6. Almost all banks offer automated transfers between your checking and savings accounts.

    7. Review your budget and check your progress every month.
